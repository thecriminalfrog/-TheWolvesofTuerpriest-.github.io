Welcome.

"There's something here in Tuerpriest, something out there in the woods. I don't know why, but its calling to me."

This is the official home of 'The Wolves of Tuerpriest', a graphic novel currently under development by creator Ananiah Bartsch. Join the new & growing community centered around 'The Wolves of Tuerpriest', a paranormal mystery rife with monsters and lurking beasts that have crept their way through history. If you think the woods of Tuerpriest are a place for you, we hope that you will feel welcome to explore our site and meet the characters of the story.

The vision for 'The Wolves of Tuerpriest was born in December of 2019. However, it wasn't until almost a year later in 2020 that it became more than a premise for art and intensive storyboarding began.

Our goal is to bring you to best story, characters and art that we possibly can, and that takes some time.

If you would like to take a look at where it all started, visit our Instagram here.
